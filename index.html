My story

I'm Alex Nginyi Kinyua — a self-driven Business Data Analyst with over 5 years of experience turning raw data into clear, actionable insights that move the business forward.

My career didn't start at a desk. I started on the ground — leading an 18-person multi-cultural team as a Valet Supervisor in Doha. That experience gave me something most analysts lack: a deep understanding of the operations that generate the data.

When I built Excel schedules that cut operational costs by 10%, it became clear that data wasn't just a reporting tool — it was a lever for real change. That insight propelled me into full-time data analysis.
